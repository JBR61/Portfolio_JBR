Portfolio

Dracula's Castle Soundscape

With this project, I was trying to convey aa world and its imagery with just sounds. I tried to make the listener feel as if they were walking through Dracula's Castle. This project was done in Audacity with sounds from freesound.org.

Saudi Cows Visual Argument

This project was made to highlight an issue that I thought needed more attention. There was an issue with water rights in Arizona, so I made this graphic for it. I used primarily gimp to put it together with images from stock photo sites. I think it portrays this issue in an effective way that could spur any internet users who came across to hopefully look deeper.

Avella Spies Save The World

This was a project made during my early years of highschool. I edited it in final cut pro, as well as wrote and directed it. In this project we had to write up a story board, as well as execute it with the props available to us and other students in the class. I chose to inclde this as I believe it showcases my creativity.
